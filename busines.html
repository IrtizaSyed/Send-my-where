Integration Cases

Pi Music Player

Pi Music Player is a beloved Android Music Player that has rich sound and simple interface. Thus far, USB connection to PC, cloud services, and emails were conventional ways to migrate music from PC to mobile. However, Pi Music Player created a new function called Pi Power Share, integrating Send Anywhere API to provide convenience in migrating music. Now, Pi Music Player users move music wirelessly using Pi Power Share.
