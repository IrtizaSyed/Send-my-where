About Us

Who we are

Send Anywhere is a global file transfer service developed/operated by Rakuten Symphony Korea based in Seoul. Send Anywhere continues to develop constantly to address the inconvenient file transfers between various devices.

What we do

Our never-ending goal is to make file transfer faster. So we relentlessly strive to simplify transfer process and optimize network connectivity.
